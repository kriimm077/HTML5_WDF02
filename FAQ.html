COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you navigate StudentHub.

01 FAQ

What is StudentHub?

StudentHub is a centralized student portal that provides access to academic information, campus events, student clubs, resources, announcements, and support services.

02 FAQ

How do I access my student dashboard?

Log in to your StudentHub account using your registered credentials. After logging in, you can access your personalized dashboard from the main navigation.

03 FAQ

Where can I check my attendance?

Your attendance information is available under the Attendance section. You can view subject-wise attendance and monitor your academic progress.

04 FAQ

How can I find upcoming campus events?

Visit the Events page to explore upcoming workshops, competitions, festivals, and other activities happening on campus.

05 FAQ

How do I join a student club?

Visit the Clubs & Communities section to explore available clubs. You can contact the respective club coordinator to learn about membership and activities.

06 FAQ

I need help with my account. What should I do?

If you are experiencing account-related issues, visit the Contact page and reach out to the StudentHub support team.
